BRÄRS

BRÄRS is ready to bugle. Are you?

Get ready to hear your favorite songs played in the style of a hundred man military brass band on steroids. Inspired by the likes of MEUTE, Lucky Chops, and Uppsala kulturskola, seven local brazzers are here to bring the new wave of brasspop to Linköping.

Guvenören

This hairy bastard is the groups self-proclaimed, non-official leader with no power. Always with something in his mouth, whether it is cigarrs or sax reeds, rumbling baritone noise is his signum.

Röde Baronen

This mad dog from the north has been released to feast upon whatever solos he can. Armed with his Martin double-bore T-bone, an instrument as simple as his mind, he is truly a forzando to be reckoned with.
